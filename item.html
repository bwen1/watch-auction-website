Description

Elegant, sophisticated and characterised by wonderfully sleek designs, the Jaeger-LeCoultre Master collection showcases stripped back models that focus purely on the essentials.

Embracing truly refined design, this Jaeger-LeCoultre Master Ultra Thin watch sits confidently within a 39mm 18ct rose gold case and fastens with a black Alligator leather strap. The charming dial is equipped with rose gold-toned hands and hour markers, as well as three chronograph sub-dials.

Powered by a reliable automatic movement and water resistant to 50 meters, a black Alligator leather strap completes the look.
